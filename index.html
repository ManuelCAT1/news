El Camino 2 is Coming

El Camino 2 is coming

#BreakingBad #ElCamino #BetterCallSaul

24 feb. 2023 8:16h.

Netflix is set to release "El Camino 2," the highly-anticipated sequel to the hit series "Breaking Bad," on March 16th. The film will pick up where the original series left off and will provide a conclusion to the story of Jesse Pinkman and his quest for freedom.

However, fans who have not yet watched the original "El Camino" film will need to act fast. Netflix has announced that the first film will be removed from the platform on March 1st, just two weeks before the release of the sequel.

"El Camino," which was released in 2019, follows the character of Jesse Pinkman as he tries to escape from his troubled past following the events of "Breaking Bad." The film received critical acclaim and was praised for its strong performances and compelling storyline.

With the release of "El Camino 2," fans of the series will finally get to see what happens to Jesse Pinkman and the other characters in the aftermath of the original series. The sequel promises to provide closure to the story and to bring the saga of "Breaking Bad" to a definitive end.

Despite the removal of the first film from Netflix, fans can still watch "El Camino" before it's too late. The film is available to stream on the platform until March 1st, giving viewers just enough time to catch up before the release of the highly-anticipated sequel.

So mark your calendars and get ready for the conclusion of one of the most beloved television series of all time. "El Camino 2" is sure to be a must-see for fans of "Breaking Bad" and anyone who loves great storytelling and compelling characters.

by: Artur Piliszek
Source: New York Times, Times, Business Insider.
